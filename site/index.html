Now, next, horizon

        
The dream and the product stay separate.

        
Every public claim needs a status. Contribution, local persistence, private backup, independent rendering, and the connection hub work locally. Production installers, mobile apps, public edge infrastructure, and chain anchoring remain unfinished.

      

      

        

          Now
          
No-wallet contribution flow

          
A person can write one thing, confirm meaning, choose visibility, save locally, export, and see public map placement when published.

        

        

          Now
          
MindLink renderer and connection hub

          
MindLink objects can be rendered independently, while the connection hub exposes current web, explorer, dashboard, compute, market, desktop, and mobile states without presenting source-only modules as finished apps.

        

        

          Horizon
          
MindChain anchoring and challenge settlement

          
Selected MindLinks can later receive durable verification records. Participation must remain useful before anchoring exists.
