Foundry registers a fixed-supply asset: the total supply you set here is minted in a single act and can never be increased or burned. In the same transaction, part of that supply is paired with your NOOS deposit to seed a constant-product pool — those two amounts define the opening price, and everything you do not deposit stays in your account.
