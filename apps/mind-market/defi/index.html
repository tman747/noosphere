Security boundary

Fail closed, not “trust the frontend.”

  • Three configured reporters; two fresh reports required; deterministic conservative median.
  • Checked integer arithmetic and bounded quantities; no floating-point consensus math.
  • Minimum locked AMM liquidity; proportional share minting; reserve product cannot decrease.
  • Collateral factors below liquidation thresholds; bounded close factor and liquidation bonus.
  • No reentrancy, callbacks, arbitrary contract calls, flash loans, or external code execution.
